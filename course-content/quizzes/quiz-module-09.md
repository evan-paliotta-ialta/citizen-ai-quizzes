# Quiz — Module 9: The Operating Framework

**Format**: Multiple choice + short answer
**Passing score**: 4/5
**Attempts allowed**: Unlimited

---

**Question 1**
You need to prepare a sensitive client-facing proposal that requires your judgment, context about the relationship, and multiple rounds of refinement. Which zone does this fall into?

A) Zone 1 — Delegate Fully
B) Zone 2 — Collaborate
C) Zone 3 — Verify and Augment
D) None — this task should not involve Claude

**Correct answer**: B

---

**Question 2**
Which of the following is a Zone 1 task?

A) A final decision on whether to renew a client contract
B) A strategic memo for the executive team
C) Formatting a meeting agenda and generating subject line options for a routine email
D) Reviewing legal terms in a vendor agreement

**Correct answer**: C

---

**Question 3**
You receive a Claude output that sounds competent but you are not sure if it actually answered your question or a slightly different version of it. Which evaluation dimension is failing?

A) Accuracy
B) Completeness
C) Relevance
D) Actionability

**Correct answer**: C

---

**Question 4**
What is the main purpose of a team prompt library?

A) To store Claude's responses for future reference
B) To share prompts that work so the team builds skill collectively rather than individually
C) To document Claude's limitations for new employees
D) To replace onboarding documentation

**Correct answer**: B

---

**Question 5 — Short Answer**
List three tasks from your current role, one for each zone. For the Zone 2 task, briefly describe what a good brief for that task would include.
