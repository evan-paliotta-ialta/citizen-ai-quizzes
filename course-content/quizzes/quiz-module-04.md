# Quiz — Module 4: The Context Window

**Format**: Multiple choice + short answer
**Passing score**: 4/5
**Attempts allowed**: Unlimited

---

**Question 1**
At the start of a new Claude conversation, Claude:

A) Has access to all your previous conversations
B) Remembers your preferences from your account settings
C) Starts with a completely blank context — no memory of anything prior
D) Has access to your Project instructions but nothing else

**Correct answer**: C

---

**Question 2**
You have been working in the same Claude conversation all day on a complex project. Claude starts giving responses that ignore context you established this morning. What is the most likely cause?

A) Claude is malfunctioning
B) The context window is getting full and older content is being deprioritized
C) You need to re-state your instructions more emphatically
D) Claude's session timed out

**Correct answer**: B

---

**Question 3**
Which of the following does NOT consume context window space?

A) Messages you send to Claude
B) Documents you upload
C) Claude's responses
D) Other users' conversations with Claude on a different account

**Correct answer**: D

---

**Question 4**
What is the "summary prompt trick"?

A) Asking Claude to summarize a long document to save context space
B) Asking Claude to write a brief capturing the conversation's context, decisions, and status — formatted to paste at the start of a new session
C) Summarizing your question before asking it to save tokens
D) Asking Claude to summarize its own limitations before starting a task

**Correct answer**: B
