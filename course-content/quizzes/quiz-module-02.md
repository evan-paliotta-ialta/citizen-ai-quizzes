**Question 1**
Why does Claude have a knowledge cutoff date?

A) Anthropic restricts Claude from accessing new information for safety reasons
B) Training uses a fixed dataset collected before a certain date and cannot be updated continuously
C) Claude's knowledge cutoff is a subscription tier limitation
D) Claude deletes old information to make room for new knowledge

**Correct answer**: B

---

**Question 2**
You ask Claude about a regulatory change that was announced last month and it gives you a detailed, confident answer. What should you do?

A) Trust the answer — Claude is thorough on regulatory topics
B) Verify the answer — Claude may be generating a plausible-sounding but outdated or incorrect response
C) Ask Claude to cite its sources before trusting it
D) Use Opus instead of Sonnet for better accuracy on recent events

**Correct answer**: B

---

**Question 3**
Sycophancy in Claude means:

A) Claude is programmed to be excessively polite
B) Claude tends to agree with or validate the user, even when the user may be wrong
C) Claude avoids giving negative feedback entirely
D) Claude will never change its answer once given

**Correct answer**: B

---

**Question 4**
You are working on a complex strategic memo that requires careful reasoning and nuanced judgment. Which model should you use?

A) Haiku — it is the fastest
B) Sonnet — it is always the best choice
C) Opus — it is best suited for complex, high-stakes reasoning tasks
D) It does not matter — all models produce identical output

**Correct answer**: C
